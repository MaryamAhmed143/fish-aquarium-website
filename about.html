Our Story

Born From a Love
of the Ocean


AquaWorld started as a small reef tank shop run by two marine biology graduates in 2008. We had one goal: to make the breathtaking world beneath the waves accessible to everyone — not just divers and oceanographers.

Over the years, we grew from a single storefront into a full-service aquarium design studio, trusted by thousands of homeowners, interior designers, hotels, and corporate offices across the country. Every tank we build carries the same passion and precision we started with.

Today, our certified team of aquarists, engineers, and designers delivers end-to-end aquarium experiences — from the first consultation to years of ongoing care — so you never have to worry about a thing.

Our Mission & Core Values

🌊

Ocean Conservation First

We source all livestock ethically and sustainably. We partner with certified coral farms and refuse wild-caught species that are at risk — because healthy oceans mean thriving aquariums.

🔬

Science-Backed Care

Every recommendation we make is grounded in marine biology. Our team of certified aquarists ensures your tank's ecosystem is biologically balanced, safe, and stunning long-term.

🎨

Design That Inspires

Lifelong Partnership

Our Services,
Explained Simply

Everything you need for a perfect aquarium — from first idea to daily upkeep. We handle the hard parts so you can enjoy the beauty.

Free Consultation

Not sure where to start? Book a free 30-minute call with one of our expert aquarists. We'll help you understand what tank size, species, and setup suits your space and lifestyle — with zero pressure.

Free · 30 min
🎨

Custom Aquarium Design

We create a full 3D render of your proposed aquarium — showing the tank size, aquascape layout, plant and coral placement, and suggested livestock. You see exactly what you're getting before we build anything.

Bespoke Design
🚚

Delivery & Professional Installation

Our team handles everything: delivery, plumbing, electrical setup, substrate laying, aquascaping, and the full nitrogen cycle. We hand over a tank ready for livestock — not a tank that needs weeks of work.

White Glove Setup
🔧

Monthly Maintenance Plans

Our aquarists visit your home or office monthly to perform water changes, clean glass and equipment, test parameters, trim plants, and check on livestock health. Your tank stays showroom-perfect year-round.

Ongoing Care
📱

Smart App Monitoring

Our connected app lets you monitor pH, temperature, salinity, and filter cycles in real time from your phone. Receive instant alerts for any issues and adjust settings remotely — peace of mind, always.

24/7 Monitoring

Emergency Callout Service

Equipment failure, sudden water quality crash, sick livestock — our emergency team is available 7 days a week to respond fast. We protect your aquarium investment and your animals when it matters most.

7-Day Response
